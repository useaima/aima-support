Frequently Asked Questions

What is eva?

Eva is a comprehensive financial assistant that helps you track spending, analyze financial behavior, and achieve your financial goals through intelligent insights and real-time anomaly detection.

How do I get started with eva?

Visit eva.useaima.com to create an account and begin your financial journey. Our onboarding process will guide you through setup and help you connect your financial accounts.

Is my data secure with eva?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your financial data. Your privacy is our top priority. Learn more at useaima.com/security.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team at help@useaima.com. We typically respond within 24 hours. You can also submit feedback directly through the eva app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. Billing is handled securely through our payment processor.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your data will be retained for 30 days in case you want to reactivate.
